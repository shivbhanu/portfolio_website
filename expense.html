Personal Expense Tracker

  • View Github Repo

Objective

Track daily expenses and build an automated report using transaction emails from the bank.

Process

  • Collect and process Gmail HTML bodies and convert them into readable text.
  • Identify patterns in body text to extract Amount, Mode, Source, and Destination of each transaction.
  • Build visualisations for lifetime expenses, savings, mode of expense, and contribution percentages.
  • Build a monthly report showing daily and cumulative amount alongside percentage of budget spent.

Key Insights

  • From July 2019 to date, total spend is approximately ₹46L across all transactions.
  • UPI accounts for ~81% of lifetime expense, followed by Credit Card (13%).
  • High-value transactions peak during daytime and evenings, with a spike on Sundays after 8 PM.
  • By transaction count, most activity happens after 8 PM on all days, peaking on Tuesdays.
